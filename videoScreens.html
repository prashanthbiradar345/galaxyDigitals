Led VideoScreens

Direct view LED video wall technology is becoming increasingly popular due to its ability to deliver seamless images, scalability to any size or shape, and excellent optimal characteristics that make video wall content look great from any angle.

We provide a wide range of LED displays from high resolution indoor video walls to solutions for the biggest outdoor public and sporting venues.

For interactive LED touch solutions, P4 LED MultiTouch is a completely seamless interactive LED video wall solution for select LED video wall lines. For high-traffic environments, P6 and P4 technology led is available for select LED video wall lines.

Advertising on the video screen is used for both short and long-term purposes, for sales promotion and for image promotion. LED screens allowed to literally take the TV out of the house. Previously, it was possible to show a video advertisement to a person only during home pastime. Now you can show high-quality advertising outside the home using the dynamic led screens. It is important that when demonstrating video advertising not on home television, but on a street video screen, the person treats it differently. In addition, people often themselves look at video screens themselves, on which they are used to seeing useful information: news, time, temperature, pressure, exchange rates etc.

The image on the LED screens can be viewed from almost any angle, which also increases the reach of the audience. Video screens offer great opportunities for advertisers to create effective advertising. It can be text, graphics, static, dynamic, black and white and colour. At the same time, video screens provide high image quality.

Our Services

With our skilled design, fabrication and national fitting teams, we can provide for all your signage requirements

CONSULTATION

Upon enquiry we will arrange a meeting to discuss your requirements. We will take details of any branding and design requirements, and discuss planning permission or landlords’ consent that you may require.

SITE SURVEY

A member of our skilled team will then visit your premises to take accurate measurements, drawup basic plans and assess fabrication requirements. Materials, fixings and, if applicable, access equipment requirements will be discussed.

DESIGN

You will then be introduced to our design team who will undertake any design for print and production using their expertise in CAD, illustration, photo manipulation. We provide mockups, samples and/or proofs of all work, and discuss fabrication options.

FABRICATION

Once the final design has been approved we’ll calculate the projected turnaround and arrange an installation/delivery date. Our design and production teams will work closely with our metalshop, wood-workers, print room, application and assembly teams; to ensure the original concept is conveyed flawlessly.

INSTALLATION

Our dedicated installation team will install your new signage and will liaise with your arranged contact to discuss any access requirements to ensure installations are carried out safely and at your convenience. We strictly adhere to all health and safety legislation.
